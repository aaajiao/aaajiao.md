## Summary

This article highlights 21 galleries making their debut at Art Basel Miami Beach in 2016, out of approximately 600 dealers who applied and 269 who were accepted. The New York Times asked Noah Horowitz, the fair's director, to highlight several notable newcomers.

The article explains the fair's structure:
- **Galleries**: The main event for top dealers from around the world
- **Nova**: For works done in the last three years
- **Positions**: Single-artist projects, an incubator for new talent
- **Survey**: Historical projects
- **Public**: Outdoor sculptures and installations in Collins Park

## Featured Galleries

### The Box, Los Angeles / Survey
- Owner: Mara McCarthy (daughter of artist Paul McCarthy)
- Featuring work by Barbara T. Smith, 85, an important feminist figure
- Part of a trend of younger galleries looking at the past

### Di Donna Galleries, New York
- Featuring Joan Miro work "Femme, oiseau, etoiles"

### Simoes de Assis Galeria de Arte, Brazil
- Showing "Cosmopolis II" (1946) by Carmelo Arden Quin
