## Summary

This article examines how technology is transforming the Chinese art market and how artists in China are embracing digital tools and platforms. The piece centers on Art Basel in Hong Kong, where Cao Fei's video game-inspired installation "Same Old, Brand New" lit up all four sides of Hong Kong's tallest building, the 108-story International Commerce Center.

The article explores several key themes:

- The rapid ascension of the Chinese contemporary art market
- How Chinese artists are using technology as both medium and subject
- The intersection of traditional artistic practices with digital innovation
- The changing landscape of art distribution and marketing in China
- The role of international art fairs in showcasing Chinese contemporary artists
