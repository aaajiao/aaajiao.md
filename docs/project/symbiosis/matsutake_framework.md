## Overview 概述

**Symbiosis** is a long-term project (2015–present) that emerged from aaajiao's decade-long collaboration with an acrylic factory in Shanghai. The project documents three intertwined forms of symbiosis: workers' meditative labor, a generation's spiritual drift, and the artist's own position within China-EU trade structures.

This document traces how the project's observations happen to resonate with Anna Lowenhaupt Tsing's analysis in *The Mushroom at the End of the World* (2015)—not as theoretical application, but as discovered parallel. The project began before the artist read Tsing; the framework clarifies what was already being observed.

**Symbiosis** 是一个长期项目（2015年至今），源于 aaajiao 与上海一家亚克力工厂的十年合作。项目记录三种交织的共生形式：工人的冥想式劳动、一代人的精神漂流、以及艺术家自身在中欧贸易结构中的位置。

本文档追溯项目的观察如何**恰好**与安娜·秦在《末日松茸》中的分析产生共鸣——不是理论应用，而是发现的平行。项目开始于艺术家阅读秦之前；这个框架只是澄清了已经在被观察的事物。

## Part I: Project Statement 项目陈述

### 共生：距离中的纠缠 | Symbiosis: Entanglement Across Distance

### 中文版

上海有一家亚克力工厂。工厂内部有一间工作室。工作室的主人住在柏林。

这不是一个悖论，而是一种结构：aaajiao 的身体在欧洲，他的生产在中国。这种分裂本身就镜像了项目所批判的贸易流动——中国制造，欧洲消费；劳动在此处，价值在彼处。艺术家没有选择掩盖这种位置，而是将它作为项目的结构性前提。

2015年，aaajiao 开始与这家工厂合作。那时没有"项目"的概念——只是需要有人切割材料。工厂的师傅们参与制作他的亚克力雕塑，这种合作关系持续了十年。2020年，他的上海工作室搬入工厂内部。工作室在那里运作，艺术家本人往返于两座城市之间。工作室的日常由工人和助手维持；艺术家的在场是间歇性的，但关系是持续的。

这种距离不是缺陷，是条件。

---

#### 第一重共生：师傅与材料

亚克力被推上台锯，刀口一进，透明里立刻起一条白雾。师傅不看温度表，只把手背轻贴边缘：烫得发黏就停刀，凉得发硬就再走一寸。抛光机嗡鸣忽高忽低，像给材料校音；粉尘落在睫毛上，机油混着午饭的葱花味，黏在喉咙里。他们很少开口，动作却互相听得见：一人松手，另一人的扳手已经递来；一人换砂纸，另一人把碎屑扫进桶里。

这样的专注，先在身体里成立，后来才被叫作**入神（Absorption）**。

入神是 **Internet Void**（aaajiao 在数字世界中追踪的算法裂隙）在物质世界的对应物：一种未被完全量化的专注，在全球化生产链的碾压下依然存在的缝隙——人与材料、人与工艺之间的连接。

---

#### 第二重共生：失落青年与城市

夜里，传说中的俱乐部进入最后几周，门口的热气裹着香水、汗与烟。低频像心跳撞进胸腔，灯光切开人群，皮肤一闪一暗。年轻人把身体交给节拍，眼神却常常不在场：有人对着镜面补妆，手机屏里仍在滚动短视频；有人在舞池里刷着同一套梗图，笑声迟到半拍。

城市曾把他们加速推上扶梯，如今下行时又把他们丢在站台。记忆被平台的回放与热搜替换，语言被表情包与模板句压扁——一种集体的**失忆与失语（amnesia / aphasia）**，像被算法温柔地按住喉咙。

aaajiao 把这种状态叫作**出神（Trance）**：身体在移动，灵魂已在别处。

入神与出神是同一种困境的两面。一个通过制作找到意义，一个通过遗忘来逃避。两种生存策略，同一种不确定性（precarity）。

---

#### 转译

当入神进入供应链，手背的判断被写成阈值：边缘温度≤62℃、噪声≤78dB、每件抛光6分钟、划痕容许值0.2mm；"唱歌"的嗡鸣变成产线日报上的曲线。注意力被折算成工时、节点、交期罚款：迟一小时，单价再压0.3%。

而出神也被转成可用的数据：停留12秒算一次"兴趣"，定位在俱乐部附近算一条"夜生活标签"，第二天推来解酒药、外卖券与同款上衣；价格被平台自动跟随，永远比同类低0.01，9.9包邮像命令。

商品跨境时又被进一步改写：申报写成"gift/样品"，单件标价刻意卡在€149.99之下，绕过 De Minimis 门槛；**通缩输出**以超低价把成本压力沿着海运、航空、末端派送一路外溢，直到某个欧洲门铃被按响——箱子轻得像无辜，里面却装着一整套看不见的劳动与治理。

手艺不再是手的故事，而是可调用的产能。

---

#### 第三重共生：远方艺术家与本地生产

aaajiao 的身体在柏林，手指在屏幕上回邮件、改图纸、确认尺寸；制作在上海，节奏在机台、手背与粉尘之间传递。文件从云端落到车间，变成切割、打磨、包装清单；作品再被贴上条码、HS编码、泡棉厚度，进入同一条跨境线路。

他并非旁观者，而是这条转译链上的一个节点：批注、确认与付款，都是推动它运转的力。他的身体在消费端，他的生产在制造端。这种分裂不是要克服的矛盾，而是要承认的结构性位置。

---

#### 共鸣

这些层叠的转换，恰好与全球贸易里"价值如何变形"的经验互相照面。项目从2015年开始，最初只是跟随制作与夜晚的现场；后来词汇才慢慢长出来，用来指认那些被压缩、被计量、又被运走的东西。是发现的平行，不是理论应用。

为什么选择纪录影像、数据可视化、游戏作为媒介？因为它们是**觉察的艺术**。纪录影像让入神与出神两种状态并置；数据可视化揭示被统计方法掩盖的青年失业率与心理健康危机；1bit 游戏让玩家在"嵌合体废墟"中漫游，按下 [SHIFT] 键进行象征性的反抗。

这些不是再现，是觉察的工具。在算法治理与政治经济的双重碾压下，觉察本身就是一种抵抗。

十年共生教会的是：**无心插柳的设计（Unintentional Design）**——多物种共同参与的世界创制，没有统一主宰者，干扰中涌现结果。aaajiao 没有设计这个项目。他被它设计了。

### English Version

There is an acrylic factory in Shanghai. Inside the factory, there is a studio. The studio's owner lives in Berlin.

This is not a paradox but a structure: aaajiao's body is in Europe, his production is in China. This split itself mirrors the trade flows the project critiques—manufactured in China, consumed in Europe; labor here, value there. The artist does not conceal this position but takes it as the project's structural premise.

In 2015, aaajiao began collaborating with this factory. There was no "project" then—he simply needed someone to cut materials. The craftsmen participated in making his acrylic sculptures; this collaboration has continued for ten years. In 2020, his Shanghai studio moved inside the factory. The studio operates there; the artist himself travels between the two cities. Daily operations are maintained by workers and assistants; the artist's presence is intermittent, but the relationship is continuous.

This distance is not a flaw. It is a condition.

---

#### First Symbiosis: Craftsmen and Material

In the acrylic factory, a craftsman checks material temperature with the back of his hand—no thermometer, just skin memory accumulated over years. The polishing wheel hums at a frequency that shifts when the pressure is right; workers describe this as "singing to the material." The workshop smells of acrylic dust, machine oil, and whatever was heated for lunch in the back room. When the rhythm is correct, no words pass between workers—hands move in synchronized patterns, consciousness dissolving into craft.

Only after these sensations does the word appear: **Absorption (入神)**.

Absorption is the material counterpart of **Internet Void** (the algorithmic fissures aaajiao traces in the digital realm): a meditative immersion where human attention and material resistance find equilibrium. This is the beauty that exists before translation—before the gesture becomes a metric, before the song becomes throughput, before meaning enters the supply chain.

---

#### Second Symbiosis: Lost Youth and the City

In the same city, aaajiao documented the final nights of a legendary Shanghai club. Bodies moved on the dance floor, but the souls had already departed. The party youth who came of age during China's boom now drift through its contraction—transformed by rapid development, then abandoned when the economy turned. Inside algorithmic echo chambers, they lose the capacity to name what is happening to them.

**Amnesia and aphasia** are not metaphors here but platform-induced social pathologies: the feed overwrites memory; the recommendation engine narrows vocabulary until experience becomes unspeakable.

aaajiao calls this state **Trance (出神)**: bodies moving, souls already elsewhere. Not chosen escape but enforced disorientation.

Absorption and Trance are two sides of the same predicament. One finds meaning through making; the other survives through forgetting. Two strategies for the same precarity.

---

#### The Transformation

The back-of-hand temperature check becomes a threshold parameter in quality control software: edge temperature ≤62°C, noise ≤78dB, polishing time 6 minutes per piece, scratch tolerance 0.2mm. The "song" of correct polishing pressure becomes a decibel reading cross-referenced with throughput curves. A craftsman's focused attention becomes labor-hours, nodes in a logistics network, delivery windows to optimize—late by one hour, unit price drops another 0.3%. Meaning becomes a cost parameter.

On the consumption side, bodies become engagement metrics. Dwell time of 12 seconds counts as "interest"; location near a club becomes a "nightlife tag"; the next day brings ads for hangover cures, delivery coupons, and matching outfits. Platform algorithms run continuous price tests, always 0.01 cheaper than competitors. 9.9 yuan free shipping feels like a command.

When goods cross borders, they are further rewritten: declared as "gift/sample," unit price deliberately set at €149.99 to slip under the **De Minimis** threshold. **Deflation export** pushes cost pressure along sea freight, air cargo, and last-mile delivery until a European doorbell rings—the box light as innocence, yet containing an entire invisible system of labor and governance.

Craft shifts from a lived rhythm to callable capacity.

---

#### Third Symbiosis: Distant Artist and Local Production

aaajiao's body is in Berlin, where requests and revisions circulate on screens; production is in Shanghai, where rhythm travels between machines and hands. Files descend from the cloud to the workshop floor, becoming cutting instructions, polishing schedules, packing lists. The finished work is tagged with barcodes, HS codes, foam thickness specifications, then enters the same cross-border routes.

He is not an external observer documenting a system—he is a node within it. Annotations, approvals, and payments are all forces that keep the chain moving. His body sits at the consumption end of the China-EU circuit; his production operates at the manufacturing end. This split is not a contradiction to resolve but a structural position to acknowledge.

---

#### Resonance

This structure—value extracted from relationships that cannot be fully designed, meaning compressed into metrics, attention converted into circulation—happens to echo observations made elsewhere about how worth transforms when entering global trade. The parallel was discovered, not imposed. The project began in 2015; the conceptual vocabulary emerged later, through watching what was already happening. Not a framework applied, but a resonance recognized.

Why documentary film, data visualization, and games as media? Because they are **arts of noticing**. Documentary juxtaposes Absorption and Trance; data visualization reveals youth unemployment and mental health crises obscured by statistical methodologies; the 1bit game lets players wander through "Chimera Ruins," pressing [SHIFT] for symbolic resistance.

These are not representations—they are tools for noticing. Under the dual crushing forces of algorithmic governance and political economy, noticing itself is a form of resistance.

What ten years of symbiosis taught: **unintentional design**—world-making through multispecies participation, no single master, outcomes emerging from disturbance. aaajiao did not design this project. It designed him.

## Part III: Critical Analysis 批判性分析

### Strongest Conceptual Connection

The strongest bridge between Tsing and Symbiosis is not "factory = forest" but **salvage accumulation under infrastructural governance**. In *Matsutake*, value is produced in the gap between **non-scalable life** (ruined forests, precarious pickers) and **scalable logistics** that turns contingency into commodity.

Symbiosis treats the acrylic factory not as a self-contained site of production, but as a **conversion interface** where meditative craft (入神) and social disorientation (出神) are both *effects of* and *resources for* larger systems: platform demand-shaping, cross-border fulfillment, De Minimis loopholes, and deflation export.

This reframes the project from "documenting labor" to **mapping how capitalism extracts value from what it does not fully design**—attention, skill, exhaustion, legal loopholes, and logistical arbitrage.

### Hidden Assumptions & Risks

| Assumption | Problem | Mitigation |
|------------|---------|------------|
| **Workers as "wild matsutake"** | Factory labor is historically the emblem of scalability (Taylorism), not non-scalability. Risk of aestheticizing precarity as authenticity. | Frame Absorption as a *residual* state within scalable systems, not an alternative to them. |
| **Ruins as natural emergence** | Symbiosis "ruins" are deliberately engineered by platform governance + trade law, not ambient decay. | Center platforms, infrastructure, and trade rules as the hidden authors of precarity. |
| **Latent commons as mutual benefit** | Artist-worker collaboration is asymmetric: artist gains authorship/cultural capital; workers gain wages but not IP or risk mitigation. | Explicitly address: Who can refuse? Who owns IP? How does visibility circulate? |
| **Salvage as primary extraction point** | For Shein/Temu, extraction happens through logistics + finance + legal arbitrage, not "wild" emergence. | Don't over-center salvage; emphasize the deliberate engineering of cheapness. |
| **Documentation as care** | Documenting workers can produce recognition but also turns gestures into content—further monetizing the depicted. | Acknowledge the camera as apparatus (Flusser); images are part of circulation, not outside it. |

### Refined Framing (Academic-Defensible)

> *Symbiosis* treats the factory not as a metaphorical forest but as an **infrastructural hinge** where non-scalable human attention is converted into scalable circulation. Drawing on Anna Tsing's account of salvage accumulation, the project tracks how value is extracted from what capitalism cannot fully design—skill, fatigue, legality, and contingency—once these are routed through platform governance and cross-border logistics.
>
> 入神 (Absorption) and 出神 (Trance) are approached as **two regulated states**: disciplined focus in production and disoriented drift in post-platform urban life, both shaped by price war and "deflation export" (通缩输出). The work positions itself as a **parallel inquiry** that extends Tsing from ecological ruins to trade-law and algorithmic infrastructures.

## Part IV: Formal Strategy 形式策略

### Traceability Inversion: A "Negative Provenance" System

Build the project around a single rule: **each object/scene must expose what normal supply-chain provenance hides**.

#### Sculpture 雕塑
- Embed machine-readable tags (QR/NFC) that *don't* authenticate luxury origin
- Instead reveal "missing fields": wage structure ranges, overtime volatility, subcontracting uncertainty, return-rate burdens, De Minimis thresholds, shipping subsidies
- Display as absences/blurred ranges rather than documentary certainty (avoid false precision)

#### Documentary 纪录影像
- Cut between gestures of Absorption (micro-precision) and logistics abstractions (route maps, tariff categories, platform price tests)
- Never as explanatory voiceover; instead as **interruptions**—the way infrastructure interrupts life while claiming to be background

#### Data Visualization 数据可视化
- Reveal what statistical methodologies obscure: youth unemployment recalculations, mental health proxies, De Minimis exploitation
- Visualize uncertainty itself, not just data points

#### Game (1bit) 游戏
- Player cannot "optimize" ethically
- Every efficiency gain increases an off-screen variable: labor invisibility, return waste, or price deflation pressure
- This operationalizes Tsing: accumulation feeds on what it cannot acknowledge

### Distance as Form: Registering the Artist's Absence-Presence 距离作为形式

The artist's Berlin-Shanghai split should not be hidden but made **structurally visible** across all media:

#### Latency as Protagonist 延迟作为主角
- Build synchronized two-column timelines (Berlin / Shanghai) where the artist appears only through **infrastructural traces**: timestamped messages, CAD revisions, QC photos, invoice terms, shipping labels, customs scans, platform metrics
- The factory appears through **labor time** and material transformation
- Refuse heroic webcam-presence substitutes; show how governance happens through protocols

#### Visualizing the Missing Body 可视化缺席的身体
- Overlay each artifact with what it governs: price, deadline, tolerance, packaging, liability
- Show measurable gaps: response delay, shipping time, revision cycles, rejected parts, sleep hours lost to time zones
- Include platform layers (recommendation spikes, return rates, De Minimis thresholds) as the invisible manager

#### Protecting Workers 保护工人
- Consent and anonymization protocols
- Avoid turning surveillance data into aesthetics
- The goal is to make the artist's privileged mobility **structurally visible** while keeping the factory from becoming mere "texture"
